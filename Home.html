My story

Hello and welcome!. As you may have seen on the home tab, my name is Juan and my Github username is @juan8834. I am a Loss Prevention Investigator for a luxury retailor. After 15 years of working retail schedules, I recently decided to make a career change into the software engineering field.

In 2009, I started working as a loss prevention investigator. The field is exciting, and I was able to grow into a management role. However I feel like I have no work life balance. I wanted to challenge myself by doing something new, something different. I want more work life balance, and I also am interested in coding. What better way than to challenge myself by learning how to code!? Please enjoy my growing portfolio, as I intend to add more projects along the way.
